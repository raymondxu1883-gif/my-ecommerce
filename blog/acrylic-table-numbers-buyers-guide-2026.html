Acrylic Table Numbers: The Ultimate Buyer's Guide for 2026

By EventDecorSigns | March 2, 2026

Table numbers are one of the most practical yet elegant elements at any wedding reception. They guide guests to their seats while adding a decorative touch to your overall theme. Among the various materials available, acrylic table numbers have emerged as the top choice for modern weddings in 2026.

Why Choose Acrylic Table Numbers?

Acrylic table numbers offer several advantages over traditional paper, cardstock, or wooden options:

  • Durability: Acrylic is shatter-resistant and can be reused for multiple events
  • Elegant Appearance: The crystal-clear surface creates a premium, modern look
  • Versatility: Can be customized with various fonts, colors, and designs
  • Weight: Lightweight yet sturdy enough to stand firmly on tables
  • Photography: Reflects light beautifully in wedding photos

Types of Acrylic Table Numbers

1. Clear Acrylic

The most popular choice. Clear acrylic numbers appear glass-like and work with any wedding color scheme. They're particularly stunning when backlit by table candles or centerpieces.

2. Frosted Acrylic

Frosted acrylic provides a softer, more muted look. It's perfect for outdoor weddings where glare might be an issue, and it pairs beautifully with rustic and vintage themes.

3. Colored Acrylic

From blush pink to gold, navy to champagne, colored acrylic adds a pop of personality. Gold and rose gold remain the most popular choices for 2026 weddings.

4. Mirrored Acrylic

For the ultimate luxury effect, mirrored acrylic reflects the surroundings and creates a glamorous ambiance. These work exceptionally well for black-tie and formal weddings.

Thin sans-serif fonts on clear acrylic. Perfect for contemporary city weddings.

Script Elegance

Cursive or script fonts in gold or rose gold. Ideal for romantic and classic weddings.

Floral Accents

Numbers enhanced with pressed flower designs or botanical illustrations.

Romeo & Juliet

Classic Roman numerals paired with elegant flourishes. Timeless and sophisticated.

How to Display Acrylic Table Numbers

  1. Stand Alone: Most acrylic numbers come with a clear stand or easel back
  2. Table Stand: Use a decorative acrylic or crystal holder
  3. Place numbers on mirrored charger plates or within centerpieces
  4. For sweetheart tables or head table display

Caring for Your Acrylic Table Numbers

One of the biggest advantages of acrylic is easy maintenance:

  • Wipe clean with soft microfiber cloth
  • Use mild soap and water solution
  • Avoid abrasive cleaners or scratchy materials
  • Store flat in protective packaging between events

Where to Buy Quality Acrylic Table Numbers

When shopping for acrylic table numbers, look for:

  • Thickness: Premium numbers use 1/8" to 1/4" acrylic
  • Edge Finish: Polished edges are safer and look better
  • Print Quality: UV-resistant printing prevents fading
  • Customization: Options for fonts, colors, and designs

Conclusion

Acrylic table numbers are an elegant, practical choice for any wedding. They photograph beautifully, coordinate with any theme, and can be reused for future events. Whether you prefer modern minimalism or classic romance, there's an acrylic style to match your vision.
