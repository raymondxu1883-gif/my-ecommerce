📝 Buyer's Guide: Photo Booth Props

Fun Photo Opportunities

Photo booth props encourage guests to let loose and create fun memories! They're a must-have entertainment element for any celebration.

Popular Prop Themes

  • Wedding: Rings, hearts, "Just Married", "Mr. & Mrs."
  • Vintage: Classic frames, vintage cars, old Hollywood
  • Modern: Neon signs, emojis, trendy designs
  • Rustic: Wood textures, flowers, nature
  • Fun: Mustaches, lips, silly glasses

Quantity Guide

  • Small (10 pcs) - Intimate gatherings (30 guests)
  • Medium (20 pcs) - Standard party (50-100 guests)
  • Large (30 pcs) - Large events (100-200 guests)
  • Extra Large (40+ pcs) - Big celebrations (200+ guests)

Tips for Best Photos

  • Set up near good lighting
  • Provide a simple backdrop
  • Keep props visible and accessible
  • Designate someone to help guests

Frequently Asked Questions

How many props do I need?
For 50-100 guests, we recommend 20-30 props. For larger events, go with 30-40+. It's better to have extras than run out!
Can I customize the props with our names?
Absolutely! We can add your names, wedding date, or custom text to many prop designs.
Are the props reusable?
Our acrylic signs are reusable. Cardstock props are designed for single-use events but can be kept as memorabilia!
How far in advance should I order?
Order at least 2 weeks before your event. Rush orders available for an additional fee.
