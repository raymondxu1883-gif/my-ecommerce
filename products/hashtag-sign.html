📝 Buyer's Guide: Wedding Hashtag Signs

Why You Need a Wedding Hashtag

A wedding hashtag makes it easy to find all photos your guests share on Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok. Instead of chasing down photos, you can simply search your hashtag after the wedding to see every moment!

Popular Hashtag Formats

  • Classic: #LastNameWeddingYear (e.g., #SmithWedding2026)
  • Couple Names: #JohnAndJane
  • Fun: #SmithsTieTheKnot
  • Initials: #JplusJ2026
  • Location: #SmithsInNashville

Choosing the Right Size

  • Small (12" x 18") - Table card holder, intimate venues
  • Medium (18" x 24") - Entrance, guest table
  • Large (24" x 36") - Photo wall, large venues
  • Extra Large (36" x 48") - Grand displays, outdoor

Display Tips

  • Place near the guest book table
  • Add to the entrance sign area
  • Include on table cards
  • Position near the photo booth
  • Announce at reception for awareness

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a wedding hashtag?
Choose something unique! Mix your names, wedding year, or fun words. Search Instagram first to make sure it's not already heavily used. Keep it simple and easy to remember!
Where should I display the hashtag sign?
Best spots: guest book table, entrance, photo booth area, or cocktail hour bar. Place it somewhere with good lighting for photos.
How far in advance should I order?
Order at least 3 weeks before your wedding. We'll send a digital proof within 24-48 hours for your approval before production.
Can I use the hashtag sign for other events?
Absolutely! Hashtag signs work great for anniversaries, birthday parties, corporate events, and more.
