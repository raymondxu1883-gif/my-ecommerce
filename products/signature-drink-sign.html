Frequently Asked Questions

How many drinks should we feature?

Most couples feature 2 signature drinks - one for each partner! You can also add a champagne toast option or a non-alcoholic drink. For a full bar, consider multiple signs.

Should we include ingredients?

Including ingredients is optional but highly recommended! It helps guests know what's in each drink and shows the thought you put into your selections. Keep it simple - main spirits and flavors.

What size do we need?

For 1-2 drinks at a small bar, 8"x10" works. For 2-3 drinks, go with 12"x18". For a full bar with multiple drinks, 18"x24" provides more space.

Can we include cocktail images?

Yes! We can add small cocktail illustrations or icons. Let us know in your order notes if you'd like graphics included with your drinks.

Can we see a proof first?

Absolutely! After you submit your quote, we'll create a digital proof showing your drinks in your chosen style. Request revisions until you love it!

📝 Buyer's Guide: Signature Drink Signs

Why Get a Signature Drink Sign?

Signature drink signs add a personal touch to your bar and help guests discover your custom cocktails. They're a fun way to showcase your personalities as a couple!

Choosing Your Signature Drinks

  • Reflect your personalities: Choose drinks you actually love
  • Match your theme: Consider your wedding colors and style
  • Keep it simple: Easy-drinking cocktails work best for crowds
  • Consider the season: Fresh drinks for summer, warm drinks for fall/winter
  • Have a backup: Make sure your bar also serves classic options

Sign Placement Ideas

  • Behind the bar: Most popular - guests see it when ordering
  • Bar entrance: Teases what's to come
  • Drink station: Near champagne or cocktail station
  • Table centerpieces: Smaller signs at tables

How Many Drinks?

  • 2 drinks: Most popular - one for each partner
  • 3 drinks: Add a champagne option or signature mocktail
  • 4+ drinks: Full bar sign for extensive options

Order Timeline

  1. 4-6 weeks before: Decide on drinks & place order
  2. 2-3 weeks before: Receive and approve proof
  3. 1 week before: Production completes
  4. 3-5 days before: Sign arrives - bring to venue!
