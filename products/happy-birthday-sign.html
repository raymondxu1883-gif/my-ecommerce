Frequently Asked Questions

Can I add a name to the sign?

Absolutely! You can choose "Happy Birthday Sarah" or any custom text. Just specify in your order notes.

What's the best size for a birthday party?

For home parties, 12"x18" or 18"x24" works great. For larger venues or outdoor parties, 24"x36" provides better visibility.

Can this be used as a photo backdrop?

Yes! Many customers place the sign behind the cake table or gift table for photos. The larger sizes work especially well for this.

Do you do themed birthday signs?

Yes! We can customize colors and designs to match your party theme. Just let us know your ideas in the order notes.

Can I see a proof before ordering?

Absolutely! After submitting your quote, you'll receive a digital proof within 24-48 hours. Request revisions until you love it!

A birthday sign makes the celebration extra special! It's a focal point for photos, guides guests, and makes the birthday person feel celebrated.
