📝 Buyer's Guide: Bar & Menu Signs

Essential Signs for Your Reception

Bar and menu signs help guests navigate your reception smoothly while adding to your decorative theme. Here's what every reception needs:

Must-Have Signs

  • Welcome Sign - Direct guests to the reception area
  • Bar Sign - Indicate open bar, cash bar, or signature drinks
  • Menu Signs - Display dinner options for each course
  • Signature Drink Signs - Name your special cocktails
  • Dessert Sign - Guide to the cake/cupcake station
  • Table Numbers - Help guests find their seats

Sign Placement Tips

  • Entrance - Welcome sign & seating chart
  • Bar Area - Bar type & drink menu
  • Dinner Tables - Table numbers & menu
  • Dessert Table - Cake cutting time & dessert sign
  • Photo Area - Hashtag & photo booth signs

Sizing Guide

  • Small (8" x 10" to 11" x 14") - Table signs, place cards
  • Medium (14" x 20" to 16" x 20") - Bar signs, menu boards
  • Large (18" x 24" to 24" x 36") - Floor signs, large displays
