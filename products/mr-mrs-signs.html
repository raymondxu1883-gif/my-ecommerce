Frequently Asked Questions

Where should I display my Mr & Mrs sign?

Popular spots include: reception entrance, sweetheart table, head table, dance floor entrance, or as home decor after the wedding. Many couples place it at their sweetheart table for photos!

Can I include our last name?

Absolutely! You can choose "Mr & Mrs" alone, or "Mr & Mrs Smith" (with your surname). Just specify in the order notes.

What's the best size for reception use?

For reception use, we recommend 18"x24" - large enough to be visible in photos but not overwhelming. For home decor, 12"x18" works beautifully.

Is the easel included?

No, easels are not included. The sign can stand on its own with the base, or be hung on a wall. We can recommend affordable display options.

Can I see a proof before ordering?

Yes! After submitting your quote, you'll receive a digital proof within 24-48 hours. Request revisions until you love your design.
