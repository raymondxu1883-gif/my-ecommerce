📝 Buyer's Guide: Wedding Seating Charts

Streamline Your Reception

A seating chart eliminates confusion and helps guests find their tables quickly. It's essential for receptions with more than 50 guests or assigned seating.

When You Need a Seating Chart

  • More than 50 guests
  • Multiple table assignments
  • Plated dinner service
  • Assigned seating
  • Complex family dynamics

Size Guide

  • Small (24" x 36") - Up to 10 tables, 100 guests
  • Medium (30" x 42") - 10-20 tables, 100-200 guests
  • Large (36" x 48") - 20-30 tables, 200-300 guests
  • Extra Large (48" x 60") - 30+ tables, 300+ guests

Design Tips

  • List guests alphabetically by table
  • Include table numbers clearly
  • Add a "General" or "Head Table" note
  • Match design to wedding theme

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I provide the guest list?
After ordering, we'll send you a simple template to fill out with guest names and table assignments. We'll create the design from there.
What size do I need?
It depends on your number of tables and guests. As a rule: 24"x36" for up to 10 tables, 30"x42" for 10-20 tables, and larger for more.
How far in advance should I order?
Order at least 3 weeks before your wedding. We need time to create the design, get your approval, and produce the chart.
Can I make changes after the proof?
We include up to 2 rounds of revisions in the price. Additional changes may incur a small fee.
