Frequently Asked Questions

How many names/words can I include?

You can include one name or a short phrase (up to 20 characters recommended). For longer text, consider a larger size.

Can I see a proof before ordering?

Absolutely! After submitting your quote, you'll receive a digital proof within 24-48 hours. We can adjust fonts, colors, and layout until you love it!

What's the best size for a nursery?

For a nursery, 18"x9" or 24"x12" works beautifully. It fits well above a crib or changing table.

Can I change colors within the design?

Yes! You can have multi-color designs. Just specify your color preferences in your order notes.

How do I care for my name sign?

Simply wipe clean with a soft, damp cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals or abrasive materials.
