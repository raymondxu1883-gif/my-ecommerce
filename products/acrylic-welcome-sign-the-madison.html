Frequently Asked Questions

How long does production take?

Once you approve your design, production takes 5-7 business days. Shipping adds another 3-5 business days. We recommend ordering at least 2-3 weeks before your event.

Can I see a proof before ordering?

Yes! After you submit your quote request, we'll create a digital proof and send it to you within 24-48 hours. You can request revisions until you love the design.

What size should I get?

For typical wedding entrances, we recommend 24"x36". For larger venues or outdoor use, consider 24"x48" or 30"x54" for better visibility.

Is the easel included?

No, easels and stands are not included. We can recommend trusted suppliers where you can purchase affordable display stands.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship worldwide. International shipping typically takes 10-14 business days.
