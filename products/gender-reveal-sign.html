Frequently Asked Questions

Should I order "Boy" or "Girl" if I don't know yet?

Order "Pink or Blue?" or "Surprise" signs that work regardless of the outcome! You can always order a new sign after the reveal if you want a "It's a Boy/Girl" sign as a keepsake.

What's the best size for a reveal party?

For holding during the moment, 18"x24" works great - big enough to see in photos but easy to hold. For display only, 12"x18" or larger works.

Can I add a holding stick?

Yes! Add a holding stick when ordering. It makes it easy to hold up the sign during the big moment for perfect photos!

Can I see a proof before ordering?

Absolutely! After submitting your quote, you'll receive a digital proof within 24-48 hours. Request revisions until you love it!

When should I order?

We recommend ordering 3-4 weeks before your reveal party to allow time for proofing, production, and shipping. Rush orders available if needed!
