📝 Buyer's Guide: Diaper Cake Toppers

Making Your Diaper Cake Special

A topper transforms a simple diaper cake into a stunning centerpiece for your baby shower. It's the perfect finishing touch that guests will notice and admire!

Topper Styles

  • Acrylic Signs: Modern, sleek, durable, can be reused as nursery decor
  • Wooden Signs: Rustic, charming, natural aesthetic
  • Paper/Cardstock: Lightweight, colorful, budget-friendly
  • Fabric Banners: Soft, flexible, whimsical

Size Guide

  • Small (6" x 8") - Small cupcakes, mini cakes
  • Medium (8" x 10") - Standard diaper cake
  • Large (10" x 12") - Large centerpieces, triple-tier cakes

Theme Ideas

  • Classic: "It's a Boy!" or "It's a Girl!" in elegant script
  • Modern: Minimalist design with gold accents
  • Nature: Florals, leaves, woodland creatures
  • Vintage: Romantic, soft colors, lace patterns

Frequently Asked Questions

What size topper do I need for my diaper cake?
For a standard 2-3 tier diaper cake, we recommend medium (8" x 10") to large (10" x 12"). The topper should be visible above the cake but not overpower it.
Can I get a custom name on the topper?
Absolutely! We can add the baby's name, parents' names, or any custom text you like. Just let us know when you request your quote.
Is the topper food-safe?
Our toppers are designed as decorative items and should not come into direct contact with food. Place it so it sits above the cake or on a pick/stem.
How far in advance should I order?
We recommend ordering at least 1 week before your baby shower. Rush orders are available for an additional fee.
