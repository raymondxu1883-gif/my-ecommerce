Confetti cannons work best! The colored confetti creates an amazing shower effect in photos. We recommend 12" or 20" confetti cannons for the best effect. Make sure to test your confetti colors beforehand!

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a stand for the backdrop?

A stand is recommended for most uses. You can add a stand when ordering, or use command strips for wall mounting. For outdoor use, we recommend adding sand weights.

What's the best size for my reveal party?

For indoor parties with 20-50 guests, 4'x6' works well. For larger venues or outdoor parties, 5'x7' or 6'x8' provides better visibility. Consider your photo angles too!

Can I use this outdoors?

Yes! Our vinyl backdrops work outdoors. We recommend using sand weights to keep it stable in wind, and avoiding direct sunlight for the best photos.

How do I set up the confetti cannon moment?

Position the reveal person(s) in front of the backdrop. Have a helper with the confetti cannon ready. When everyone is ready, POP! Make sure your camera is on video mode!

Can I see a proof before ordering?

Absolutely! After submitting your quote, you'll receive a digital proof within 24-48 hours showing your design. Request revisions until you love it!
