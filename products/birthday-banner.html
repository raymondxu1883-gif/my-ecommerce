Frequently Asked Questions

How do I hang the banner?

The banner comes with grommets (metal rings) in each corner. Use the included ties, or use rope, hooks, or Command hooks to hang. It's easy!

Can I use the banner outdoors?

Yes! Our vinyl banners are weather-resistant and can be used outdoors. Just make sure it's securely attached in windy conditions.

What's the best size for my party?

For home parties, 5ft works great. For larger venues or outdoor parties, 6ft-8ft provides better visibility. Consider your space!

Can I add a name to the banner?

Absolutely! You can add "Happy Birthday [Name]" or any custom text. Just specify in your order.

Can I see a proof before ordering?

Yes! After submitting your quote, you'll receive a digital proof within 24 hours showing exactly how your banner will look.

📝 Buyer's Guide: Birthday Banners

Why Get a Birthday Banner?

A birthday banner is the easiest way to transform any space into a birthday celebration! It's visible, festive, and makes an amazing photo backdrop!

Choosing the Right Size

  • 3ft: Small spaces, doorways, cake table
  • 5ft: Home parties, backyard gatherings
  • 6ft: Medium venues, restaurant parties
  • 8ft: Large events, outdoor parties

Best Placement Spots

  • Behind the cake table - Perfect photo spot!
  • Behind the gift table - Guests see it when arriving
  • Entrance/doorway - First impression!
  • Behind the birthday person - For group photos

Color Suggestions

  • Gold: Elegant milestone birthdays
  • Rose Gold: Romantic, trendy
  • Pink: Fun, feminine parties
  • Blue: Masculine, classic
  • Rainbow: Kids parties, fun vibes
