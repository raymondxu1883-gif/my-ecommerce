Frequently Asked Questions

How long does production take?
Production takes 3-5 business days after design approval. Shipping adds another 2-5 business days for US orders.
Can I see a proof before ordering?
Yes! After you submit your quote request, we'll create a digital proof and send it to you within 24-48 hours. You can request revisions until you love the design.
What size should I get?
For indoor parties, we recommend 18"x24" or 24"x36". For outdoor use or large venues, consider 30"x42" for better visibility from a distance.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes! We ship worldwide. International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days.
