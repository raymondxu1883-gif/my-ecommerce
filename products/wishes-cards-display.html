Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work?
Guests take a card, write their message, seal it in an envelope, and drop it in the collection box. After the wedding, you can read all the messages together!
Can I customize the prompts?
Absolutely! We can customize prompts to reflect your relationship, ask specific questions, or use your own ideas.
How many cards do I need?
We recommend 1 card per guest plus extras. If you expect 100 guests, order at least 100 cards to ensure everyone can participate.
Is there a way to display the cards after?
Yes! Many couples create a shadow box or album to display their favorite cards after the wedding.
