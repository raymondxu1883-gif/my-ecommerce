Frequently Asked Questions

What should I write on the guest book sign?

Classic options include "Guest Book", "Please Sign Our Guest Book", or "Leave Us Your Wishes". You can also customize with your names or wedding date!

Do I need a physical guest book?

Yes! The sign guides guests to a physical book. You can use a traditional bound book, a sign-in poster, or even blank pages in a scrapbook.

What size should I get?

For table placement, 8"x10" or 12"x18" works well. For floor standing or wall display, 18"x24" provides better visibility.

Can the sign stand on its own?

Yes! Our signs have a free standing base. For larger signs, consider using an easel for extra stability.

Can I see a proof before ordering?

Absolutely! After submitting your quote, you'll receive a digital proof within 24-48 hours. Request revisions until you love it!
