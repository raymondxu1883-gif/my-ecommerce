📝 Buyer's Guide: Dance Floor Decals

Add Magic to Your Dance Floor

A dance floor decal creates a stunning focal point and amazing photo opportunities! It's one of the most impactful decorative elements for weddings and events.

Popular Designs

  • Names + Date: "John + Jane • June 15, 2026"
  • Monogram: Combined initials (J & J)
  • Pattern: Chevron, stars, hearts, flowers
  • Quote: "Dancing Since 2026"
  • "Just Married" Dance: Special last dance decal

Sizing Guide

  • Small (18") - Small events, photo corners
  • Medium (24") - Standard 12x12 dance floor
  • Large (36") - Large dance floor areas
  • Extra Large (48") - Grand entrances, large venues

Floor Compatibility

  • Works Best: Tile, sealed concrete, hardwood
  • Use Caution: Uneven surfaces, grout lines
  • Not Recommended: Carpet, outdoor grass

Frequently Asked Questions

Will the decal damage my floor?
No! Our premium vinyl decals are designed to be removable. They leave no residue when properly removed according to instructions.
Can I use the decal outdoors?
Our decals are designed for indoor use on flat surfaces. Outdoor use is not recommended as weather and uneven ground can affect adhesion.
How do I know what size to order?
Measure your dance floor area. For a standard 12x12 dance floor, we recommend 24" size. For larger floors, go with 36" or 48".
How far in advance should I order?
We recommend ordering at least 2 weeks before your event to allow time for design proof and production.
