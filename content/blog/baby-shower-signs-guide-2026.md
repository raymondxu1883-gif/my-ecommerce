# Baby Shower Signs: The Complete Guide for 2026

Baby showers are joyous celebrations marking the upcoming arrival of a little one. The right signs and decorations create the perfect atmosphere for this special event while helping guests navigate the party.

## Why Baby Shower Signs Matter

Signs do more than decorate—they guide, inform, and set the mood:

- **Welcome guests** to the celebration
- **Direct traffic** to gift table, food, games
- **Create photo opportunities** everyone loves
- **Capture the theme** and anticipation of the new baby

## Trending Baby Shower Sign Styles in 2026

### 1. Gender Reveal Signs

The most exciting element of modern baby showers!

**Popular Options:**
- "Boy or Girl?" reveal sign with confetti
- "It's a Boy/Girl" celebration sign
- Confetti cannon-compatible designs

### 2. Welcome Signs

Set the tone immediately:

**Essential Information:**
- "Baby [Last Name] Shower"
- Date and time
- Host names (if applicable)
- Venue directions

**Trending Styles:**
- Soft pastels (blush pink, sage green, lavender)
- Floral-accented designs
- Modern minimalist with gold accents

### 3. Gift Table Signs

Help guests find the perfect spot:

- "Gifts & Cards" header
- Instructions (gift vs. envelope)
- Registry information (subtle)

### 4. Food & Drink Signs

Elevate your refreshment table:

- "Munchies for Mom-to-Be"
- Gender-coded drink stations
- Cake/cupcake topper signs

### 5. Advice & Wishes Signs

Interactive elements guests love:

- "Advice for the Mama-to-Be"
- "Wishes for Baby"
- Guestbook alternative signs

## Color Trends for 2026

### Popular Palettes:

**Soft & Gender-Neutral**
- Blush pink + sage green + cream
- Lavender + soft yellow + white
- Mint + coral + tan

**Bold & Gender-Reveal**
- Blue + navy + silver (Boy)
- Pink + gold + burgundy (Girl)
- Rainbow confetti (Surprise!)

## Baby Shower Sign Themes

- **Classic Boy**: Navy blue, gray, white, storks, baby blocks
- **Classic Girl**: Blush pink, rose gold, butterflies, flowers
- **Modern Minimalist**: Clean lines, simple typography, metallic accents
- **Woodland/Nature**: Earth tones, forest animals, trees
- **Floral & Garden**: Watercolor florals, garden roses, soft romantic
